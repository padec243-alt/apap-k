# **App Name**: APAP-K

## Core Features:

- Homepage Dual CTA: Prominent call-to-action buttons on the homepage directing users to either the 'Actions au Kasaï (RDC)' or 'Actions à Lyon' sections.
- Filterable Project Portfolio: A portfolio on the 'Actions au Kasaï' page where projects can be filtered by category (Eau, Agriculture, Femme, etc.).
- Artist Showcase: Dedicated pages to highlight artists in the 'Actions à Lyon' section, featuring biographies and links.
- Dynamic Newsfeed: A dynamic feed displaying news, updates, and articles to keep the community informed and engaged.
- AI Content Enhancement tool: Generative AI feature that serves as a tool for suggesting improvements to written content.
- Prominent Donation Buttons: Clearly visible 'Donate' buttons persistently in the main menu to encourage financial support.
- Multilingual Support: Feature to support content in both French and another language, automatically detecting and routing the user.

## Style Guidelines:

- Primary color: Congo Blue (#4682B4) evoking stability, professionalism, and connection to the Democratic Republic of Congo.
- Background color: Light Gray (#F0F8FF), a desaturated version of Congo Blue, to give a modern look that is easy on the eyes.
- Accent color: French Red (#F44336) draws on the colors of the French flag and brings an element of energy and visual contrast.
- Headline font: 'Belleza' sans-serif with personality for a style aligned to fashion, art, and design; good for headlines and short amounts of text
- Body font: 'Alegreya' humanist serif with an elegant, intellectual, contemporary feel
- Modern and minimalist icons that represent different sections and functionalities.
- Clean and well-structured layout that provides an intuitive navigation experience.
